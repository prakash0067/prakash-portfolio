About This App

Inventra

Inventory Management & Billing App

Inventra is a modern Android-based application designed to help businesses manage their products, sales, staff, and billing all in one place. It offers seamless product tracking, barcode and voice-based search, real-time analytics, and invoice generation. Built with a clean UI and robust backend, Inventra simplifies inventory handling through smart automation, low-stock alerts, and user-friendly input features.
